About HairRecommender

A smart, privacy-first AI tool that analyses your face shape and personal preferences to recommend the most flattering hairstyles — built entirely from scratch with a custom CNN and cosine-similarity engine.

🧠 Custom CNN Model A convolutional neural network trained from scratch classifies your face shape into oval, round, square, heart, or oblong with high confidence.
📊 Cosine Similarity Matching Each hairstyle is encoded as a feature vector. Weighted cosine similarity ranks every style against your unique face & preference vector.
👀 Computer Vision Hair Analysis MediaPipe landmarks combined with image segmentation automatically detect your current hair length and type directly from the photo.
♀️♂️ Gender-Aware Recommendations Recommendations and “styles to avoid” are always filtered to match your selected gender — no cross-gender results, ever.
🔒 Privacy First: We never store your photos. All image processing happens entirely on the server during your request and is permanently deleted the moment your results are returned. No account required.
